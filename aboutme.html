About Me

Matt Costa

Hi there! I'm Matt! A long time ago I wanted to become a filmmaker. After picking up an old 8mm video camera, I was hooked on the idea of telling stories for a living. Flash forward 6 years of college, and 8 years of working in the real world, and I kinda gave up on the dream. Between the highly competitive nature of selling creative works. And dedicating the time to the skill of effective storytelling. The principles I learned over the years of attempting to create my own works of art still linger in my mind. I can't watch movies anymore without deconstructing the elements and language used in them every day. It's a gift, and a curse. I designed this website as a means to leave these skills to you, dear reader. However you so chose to do it. This is a digital monument to everything I've learned.

Professionally I have worked with companies like Pepsi, Reebok, & CrossFit to name a few. And I am currently in the works on a handful of screenplays I have slowly developed over time. As well as a full length book.
