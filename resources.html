Top Books on Screenwriting

I would argue that most, if not all of these books have been crucial to my writing approach. Blake Snyder and Michael Hauge give you a good grasp of sales. Syd Field and Robert McKee on technique. And so on. So fourth. (note: these will be affiliate links, helps keep the lights on)
  1. Syd Field's Screenplay
  2. Robert McKee's Story
  3. Michael Hauge's Writing Screenplays That Sell
  4. Christopher Volger's The Writer's Journey
  5. Blake Snyder's Save The Cat!
  6. William Akers' Your Screenplay Sucks!
  7. Lajos Egri's The Art of Dramatic Writing
